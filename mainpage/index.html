21Rush

Hi,Welcome to 21 Rush!

Get ready for an exciting game of blackjack where the goal is to reach a total card value of 21 without going over.

Rules of the Game:

  1. The game is played with a standard deck of 52 cards represented by numbers.
  2. Face cards (King, Queen, Jack) have a value of 10.
  3. Number cards are worth their face value.
  4. Aces can be worth 1 or 11, depending on which value benefits the hand more.
  5. The dealer will deal two cards to each player, including themselves.
  6. Players take turns deciding whether to 'hit' (take another card) or 'stand' (keep their current total).
  7. The goal is to have a hand value as close to 21 as possible without exceeding it.
  8. If a player's total exceeds 21, they 'bust' and lose the game.
  9. The dealer must hit until their total is 17 or higher.
  10. The player with a hand closest to 21 without busting wins.
